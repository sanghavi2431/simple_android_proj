11. Cookies

Cookies are widely used in order to make websites work, or work more efficiently. When you visit our Website, we collect some of your Personal Information transmitted to us by your browser via cookies or similar tracking technologies. This enables you to access Woloo Platform and helps us to create better user experience for you. In the process, Woloo uses cookies, beacons, clear gifs, pixel or similar tracking technologies by third-parties for the purpose of online behavioral advertising, end user targeting and cross-device linking and to assist in delivering the service and to provide a positive and personalized user experience. Cookies are files sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer’s hard drive. Our persistent and session ID Cookies are used to identify unique visitors and to provide a personalized user experience. Beacons are bits of code that function similar to cookies. Our beacons are embedded in outgoing emails and generate a call back to the Woloo server when such emails are opened. Woloo uses beacons to track the status of sent emails. If you do not wish to receive cookies, you may disable them. A pixel is a URL used in a creative to record an impression in an advertiser’s system. Pixels may be added to advertisements served on Woloo to record that they are seen by the user.
